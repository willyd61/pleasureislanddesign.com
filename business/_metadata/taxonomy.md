# Business Document Taxonomy & Metadata

## Core Categories

- **marketing**: All promotional, social, advertising, content strategy, hashtag research, campaign performance.
- **operations**: Day-to-day processes, customer experience, vendor management, internal checklists, welcome/onboarding materials.
- **strategy**: High-level planning, financials (redacted where necessary), growth roadmaps, competitive positioning, annual/quarterly plans.
- **people-hiring**: Talent acquisition, role definitions, onboarding frameworks, team performance (anonymized).
- **design-brand**: Visual identity, guidelines, logo history, photography standards, moodboards.

## Subcategory Examples

**marketing/**: campaigns, social, instagram, ads-google, email, flyers, roi-analysis
**operations/**: customer-journey, vendors, sop, checklists, service-standards, welcome-packet
**strategy/**: annual-plan, quarterly-review, roadmap, audit, business-model, financial-projection
**people-hiring/**: job-description, hiring-report, onboarding, culture
**design-brand/**: guidelines, logo-evolution, photography, before-after-standards

## Why This Taxonomy?

Aligned with small service business org chart and website sections (services, gallery, specials, blog, about). Enables easy mapping to MkDocs nav and future internal tools. Scalable without over-fragmentation.

## Evolution

Propose changes via PR to this file + update business/README.md. AI can suggest new subcats based on document content.
