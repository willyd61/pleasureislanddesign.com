Wilmington FAQ

Common Questions

Questions about our service in the Wilmington area? Don't see yours below? Contact us directly — we're happy to help.

Do you serve all of Wilmington?

Yes — we serve the entire City of Wilmington and surrounding communities, including Mayfaire, Landfall, Porters Neck, Ogden, Monkey Junction, Murrayville, and the Wrightsville Beach area. If you're unsure whether your neighborhood is within our service area, give us a call at (910) 444-1230 and we'll confirm right away.

How far do you travel for Wilmington projects?

Our primary service area covers greater Wilmington, the Cape Fear coast (Kure Beach, Carolina Beach), and the Brunswick County area including Leland. We are based in Wilmington and do not charge travel fees for projects within New Hanover County. For projects further out, contact us and we'll work something out.

How soon can you start my Wilmington project?

Lead times vary with the season. Spring and summer are our busiest periods in Wilmington, so we recommend reaching out 4–6 weeks in advance if possible. During slower seasons, we can sometimes start within 1–2 weeks of your consultation. Request a consultation to get a current availability estimate.

Do you offer free estimates in Wilmington?

Absolutely. All consultations and estimates are completely free and come with no obligation. We'll visit your home, assess your cabinets, discuss your vision, and provide a detailed written quote — all at no cost to you.

What is the process for a Wilmington project?

After your free consultation, we schedule your project start date. We arrive, carefully remove and tag every cabinet door, protect your kitchen surfaces, and take the doors to our dedicated spray booth facility. We complete in-home frame work simultaneously. Most Wilmington projects are fully complete and cabinets reinstalled in 5–8 business days.
